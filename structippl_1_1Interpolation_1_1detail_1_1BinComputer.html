Detailed Description

template<unsigned Dim, typename RealType>
struct ippl::Interpolation::detail::BinComputer< Dim, RealType >

Functor to compute bin index for a particle position.

Maps physical particle positions to tile indices for tiled scatter/gather. Particles are assigned to tiles based on their stencil center location.
