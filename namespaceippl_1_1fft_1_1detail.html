CUDA kernel that transposes a 3D buffer from LayoutLeft to LayoutRight indexing.

LayoutLeft: src[i + j*n0 + k*n0*n1] (i is fastest-varying). LayoutRight: dst[k + j*n2 + i*n1*n2] (k is fastest-varying).
