Detailed Description

template<typename Field, typename Tag>
class ippl::fft::TrigBase< Field, Tag >

Shared implementation for sine / cosine transforms.

Holds a HeffteTrig backend, an internal LayoutLeft scratch buffer, and the FFTW-only renormalization (FFTW emits trig outputs scaled by 8 in 3D; other backends do not).

Template Parameters
FieldIPPL Field of real elements.
TagOne of SineTransform, CosTransform, Cos1Transform.
