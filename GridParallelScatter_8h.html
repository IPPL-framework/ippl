Detailed Description

Output-tile-parallel scatter (one team per output tile).

Particles must be pre-binned by output tile. Each Kokkos team owns one output tile, walks its bin and accumulates into team scratch, then flushes to global memory once. Lower atomics traffic than AtomicScatter at high ppc; higher launch cost than TiledScatter for tiny tiles.
