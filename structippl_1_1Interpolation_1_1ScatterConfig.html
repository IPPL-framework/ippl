Default constructor - initializes tile sizes based on Dim.

3-D defaults are intentionally conservative: the shared-memory footprint of the scatter histogram grows as (tile + W + 1)^3 * sizeof(RealType) * (2 if complex) For the largest supported kernel width (W = 14) and complex<double>: (2 + 14 + 1)^3 * 8 * 2 = 17^3 * 16 ~= 78 KB – fits in 96 KB L1. (3 + 14 + 1)^3 * 8 * 2 = 18^3 * 16 ~= 93 KB – marginal. Tile = 2 is therefore the safe 3-D default. Scatter::dispatch() further reduces the tile at runtime if the resolved config would still exceed the device's available shared memory.
