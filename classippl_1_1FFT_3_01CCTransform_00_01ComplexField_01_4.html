Detailed Description

template<typename ComplexField>
class ippl::FFT< CCTransform, ComplexField >

In-place complex-to-complex FFT over a complex IPPL Field.

Selects cuFFTMp when IPPL_ENABLE_CUFFTMP is defined, otherwise the heFFTe C2C backend. The transform is performed on a contiguous LayoutLeft scratch view; ghost cells are stripped on the way in and restored on the way out.

Template Parameters
ComplexFieldField whose value_type is a Kokkos::complex.
