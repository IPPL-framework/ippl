Detailed Description

template<typename RealField>
class ippl::FFT< RCTransform, RealField >

Real-to-complex / complex-to-real FFT over IPPL Fields.

Forward transforms a real field into its half-complex spectrum, the backward transforms back. Selects cuFFTMp on CUDA-MP builds, otherwise the heFFTe R2C backend.

Template Parameters
RealFieldIPPL Field of real-valued elements.
